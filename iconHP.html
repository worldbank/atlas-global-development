Data stories on global development

In 2026, the World Bank Atlas of Global Development focuses on progress. When we talk about global development, how do we define, evaluate and talk about progress? How fast can we expect countries in different situations, starting points and contexts to progress towards a better future? We have developed a novel framework for measuring progress in this edition of the Atlas. Read stories under five themes or explore how fast countries have progressed across various indicators.
